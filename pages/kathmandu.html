Swayambhunath Temple

Swayambhunath is a glorious ancient religious architecture, located in the middle of Kathmandu city. It is center of faith of both Buddhist and Hindus.

Duration: 3 - 4 hours Location: Swoyambhu, Kathmandu, Nepal Entrance Fee: NPR 200 Opening Hours: 24 hours

Bhaktapur Durbar Square

The ancient city Bhaktapur lies on the Eastern part of Kathmandu valley which is also known as Bhadgaon or Khwopa. The historical monument on around signifies medieval age culture and tradition of Nepal and this old city is inhabited by indigenous Newari people in large group. you can visit to this place to experience Nepali culture,tradition,religion from right way.we are here to support you for your tours.

Duration: 5 - 6 hours Location: Durbar Square, Bhaktapur, Nepal Entrance Fee: NPR 1000 Opening Hours: 9 am - 6 pm

Narayanhiti Palace Museum

Narayanhiti Royal Palace Museum in Kathmandu unveils Nepal's regal history through ornate halls and curated exhibits of royal artifacts. Explore opulent living quarters, ceremonial spaces, and a collection of historical treasures, offering a glimpse into the country's royal heritage.

Duration: 3 hours Location: Narayanhiti Path, Kathmandu, Nepal Entrance Fee: NPR 1000 Opening Hours: 10:30 am - 3:30 pm

Pashupatinath Temple

Pashupatinath Temple, nestled along the Bagmati River, is a sacred sanctuary in Kathmandu. A UNESCO World Heritage site, it blends spirituality and ancient rituals, offering a serene space for reflection amidst the cultural richness of Nepal.

Duration: 2 hours Location: Pashupatinath, Kathmandu, Nepal Entrance Fee: NPR 1000 Opening Hours: 4 am - 9 pm

Kopan Monastry

Kopan Monastery, nestled in serene surroundings, is a tranquil Tibetan retreat near Kathmandu. Embrace the peaceful ambiance, engage in meditation, and immerse yourself in Tibetan Buddhist wisdom at this spiritual haven.

Duration: 2 - 3 hours Location: Budhanilkantha, Kathmandu, Nepal Entrance Fee: Free Opening Hours: 10 am - 4 pm
